Introduction

Population of brazil

According to the Continuous PNAD, a sample survey carried out annually by the IBGE, the Brazilian population in 2021 was 213,317,639 people.

Turnout

Number of registered voters

155,900,451 people were registered to vote in 2022

Number of people who voted

The turnout in the first round is 79.07% and 79.43% in the second

Justification, transit and registration

Number of justifications

4.437% of the voters justificated their presence in the first round of 2022 elections, while 4.152% did this in the second round.

Number of transit

In the first round, 332,547 were able to vote in transit. Of these, 7.728% abstained on voting day. In the second round, 314,803 were able to vote in transit. Of these, 9.582% abstained on voting day
